Project
I developed an IoT-based Vehicle Theft Prevention System that integrates Facial Recognition and RFID technology to enhance security. The system ensures that only authorized individuals can access and operate the vehicle. Facial recognition is used to verify the identity of the driver, while the RFID tag acts as an additional layer of authentication. If either verification fails, the system prevents the vehicle from starting and sends an alert to the owner. This project demonstrates the practical application of IoT and AI in improving vehicle security.
